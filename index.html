ESSEX RANGERS YFC

Essex Rangers YFC was founded in 2019 and was formerly known as Barkingside Youth FC.

We provide football across ALL abilities and backgrounds for children aged between U7 to U18 and we currently field over 20 teams across these age groups. We also work with SoccerKids soccer school to accommodate children younger than U7.

We are very much 'ONE CLUB' and NOT a group of teams and we will continue to exist in this fashion building unity throughout, not steering away from our values of how we attempt to make our members experience a positive and healthy one.

We work hard to give players an understanding of the game through planned practise, working closely with coaches and managers to transfer this onto the game day experience.

Our excellent facilities create a fantastic healthy learning environment for ALL players to enhance their ability and maximise their potential We are constantly looking to excel and expand in all areas of the club and invite players/teams/sponsors who want to be a part of our club to CONTACT US NOW!

What we can provide when joining our club:

Teams for ALL ages including U6s

Weekend and Midweek Training throughout the year including school holidays

Coaching from Level 1/2/Uefa B Qualified Coaches

Coaching from Coaches CRB Checked, Safeguarding and First Aid Trained

Latest Training/Matchday equipment

Full Playing Kit Personalised Training Kit Package

End of Season Team Awards Presentation

Summer Tournaments Club Events

Full Public Liability & Personal Accident Insurance ​
